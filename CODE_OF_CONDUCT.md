# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Participation in this project should be respectful, constructive, and professional.

Examples of expected behavior:

- assuming good intent
- giving actionable technical feedback
- discussing disagreements on the merits
- being careful with security reports and sensitive information
- helping keep the project usable for both human operators and agent-driven workflows

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- publishing private information without permission
- submitting malicious code, credentials, or exploit payloads outside responsible disclosure
- derailing technical discussions with hostility or bad-faith argument

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation for behavior that harms the project or its contributors.

## Reporting

For ordinary community issues, open a normal repository issue or discussion if available.

For security-sensitive misconduct or secret exposure, follow the process in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) and avoid public disclosure until maintainers can respond.
